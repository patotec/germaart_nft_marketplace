WELCOME TO APETOPIA


The Year is 2X99. Facing extinction due to a lack of resources, food and water, the Apes rebeled against their human overlords, seizing the technology as their own. Will you be their patron and help them conquer the Galaxy?

The ZKApes Nft is a procedurally generated collection of 6666 ERC-721A NFTs on the Ethereum blockchain, with more than 100 hand-drawn unique traits inspired in science fiction and pop culture.

This new ERC-721 standard allows for cheaper gas transactions on both single and multiple batch mints, helping you save on your NFT ventures.

Our focus lies in creating an inclusive, familiar and strong community for all the fellow apes out there, and pioneer the Metaverse together. In doing so, we want to break down the walls between members from different backgrounds by empowering awesome artists and helping the Ape culture expand to Infinity... and Beyond!
